FAQ

TravelEZ is a vacation planner app that allows users to find their dream vacation.

Our application can be used on any device and enables ease of use through features like sorting and tab navigation.

You can search destinations, hotels, or flights or you can receive a customized vacation recommnedation from our quiz.

Navigate to the search page. Select the Location Search Icon, and enter the city you want to visit.

Navigate to the search page. Select the Hotel Search Icon , and enter the city you want to visit along with your check-in and check-out dates.

Navigate to the search page. Select the Flight Search Icon , and enter the city you want to visit and your travel date. We even have a round trip option!

Under the text fields for entering a destination, make sure you have entered a valid city. If your error persists, we do not have any data on that destination.
